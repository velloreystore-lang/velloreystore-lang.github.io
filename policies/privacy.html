Privacy Policy – Vellorey.com

Updated: 18 November 2025

At Vellorey.com, we value your privacy and work hard to protect any information you share with us. Moreover, we keep our practices clear and simple so you always understand how your data is handled.

When you visit our site, we may collect basic details such as your browser type, pages viewed, and general usage patterns. Although this helps us improve your experience, we never use it to personally identify you.

If you contact us or submit content, we may store your name, email, or message for communication purposes. In addition, we use this information only to reply, assist, or manage your submissions.

We also offer visitors the chance to write and submit articles for publication on Vellorey.com. Therefore, any content you provide may be reviewed, edited, or shared publicly after your permission, ensuring a smooth and positive process.

Cookies may be used to personalize your browsing experience. Even so, you can disable cookies anytime through your browser settings. This may slightly affect site performance, but your control remains fully intact.

At times, we may share links to third-party websites for your convenience. However, we cannot control their policies, so we encourage you to review their terms before interacting with them.

We never sell your personal information, and we avoid unnecessary data collection. Furthermore, we use secure methods to protect your details from misuse or unauthorized access.

By using Vellorey.com, you agree to this Privacy Policy and understand how your information may be used. If our policy changes, we will update this page so you stay informed.
